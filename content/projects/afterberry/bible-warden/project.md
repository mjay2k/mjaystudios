A gamified Bible reading app for iOS — designed, branded, and built by me in Swift with Xcode and Claude. Streak tracking, XP progression, and Chapter Battles that quiz you before you move on. The goal: make daily reading addictive, not obligatory. Backend on Google Firestore, with a full brand identity and animated logo mark I'm particularly proud of.

---extended---
Bible Warden started with a simple problem — keeping a consistent reading habit. The solution turned into a full product: quest-based progression through Scripture, Chapter Battles that test comprehension before unlocking the next chapter, inventory and highlights systems, and deep customization with multiple themes and typography options. Beyond the core reading experience, the app includes mini games — Swordle, a Bible-themed Wordle, and Triune, a daily puzzle game tied to a daily Bible verse. Every screen, interaction, icon, and the brand identity itself — designed and built from scratch. The companion site at biblewarden.com was also vibe-coded by me. Available now on the App Store.
