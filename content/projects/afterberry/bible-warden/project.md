A gamified Bible reading app built in Swift for iOS. Streak tracking, reading plans, and progress visualization designed to make daily reading a habit — not a chore.

---extended---
Bible Warden started as a personal project to solve a real problem — keeping a consistent Bible reading habit. Built natively in Swift, the app features streak tracking, customizable reading plans, and progress visualization. The UI is clean and purposeful, designed by the same eye that art directed national campaigns. Every screen, transition, and interaction was designed and built by me from scratch.
