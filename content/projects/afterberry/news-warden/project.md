A custom news aggregator I designed and built — AI-curated stories, ongoing story tracking, and a reading experience that cuts through the noise. Supabase backend, Vercel hosting, local and cloud AI models handling curation.

---extended---
News Warden pulls from curated sources and uses AI at every layer — a local model on my Mac Mini ingests stories throughout the day, while a larger model on OpenRouter runs multiple passes for curation, scoring, and content polish. Features like Ongoing Stories aggregate coverage around developing events, and AI Warden scores AI news by real-world risk and opportunity. I directed the editorial architecture and iterated on the AI pipeline with Claude until the output felt right. Built to be the news app I actually wanted to use.
