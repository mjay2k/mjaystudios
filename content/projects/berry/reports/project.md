Four years of Berry Global's flagship Impact Report — large-scale interactive PDFs (43 to 119 pages) featuring custom data visualization, infographics, and editorial design. Each report includes a clickable table of contents for easy navigation across sections covering environmental, social, and governance initiatives.

---extended---
As Art Director, I designed and built the vast majority of Berry Global's annual Impact Report from 2020 through 2023. These are substantial corporate publications — ranging from 43 pages (2020) to 119 pages (2022) — combining data-heavy sustainability reporting with polished editorial design. Each report features custom infographics, data visualizations, photography direction, and a clickable table of contents for navigation. The 2022 edition was co-art directed with another designer, but I remained the primary designer who built the full project. The back half of each report is information-dense governance and data content, while the front sections showcase Berry's environmental and social impact through designed editorial spreads.
