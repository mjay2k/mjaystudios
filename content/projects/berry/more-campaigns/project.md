A collection of trade publication campaigns art directed for Berry Global. Each ran across industry publications like Plastics Industry Magazine and Plastics Today, with some extending into email, digital ads, and direct mail. From barrier packaging technology to recycled resin leadership to greenhouse film products — each campaign distills a complex B2B value proposition into a single, striking visual.

---extended---
Four campaigns spanning Berry Global's diverse product portfolio, each tailored for trade publication audiences. The Protect campaign uses an oyster guarding its pearl as a visual metaphor for Berry's barrier packaging technology. Verdant Goodbye showcases premium packaging made with high post-consumer resin (PCR) content — the ad shows the product in a hero shot and being recycled simultaneously, selling the dual life of sustainable packaging. Looking for More targets the European market with Berry's CleanStream recycled resin technology, positioning Berry as offering more recycled content options than competitors. Growing Season Grows promotes Berry's Tufflite greenhouse films with a clever extended calendar — October stretching to day 71 — demonstrating that the product literally pays for itself through longer growing and harvesting seasons.
