Four-part brand values campaign — Engaging, Protective, Innovative, Committed. Each ad pairs bold typography with product photography to communicate Berry's core identity across trade publications.

---extended---
Berry needed a unified campaign that could communicate its brand pillars across trade publications and internal communications. I designed a system where each value — Engaging, Protective, Innovative, Committed — gets its own ad with distinct imagery but a consistent layout framework. The campaign reinforced Berry's position as a Fortune 500 packaging leader.
