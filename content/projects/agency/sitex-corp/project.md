Designed and co-developed a website for SiteX Corp, a professional clothing company. The site lets users swap the background pattern between three animal prints — a direct tie-in with their "Dress for Your Role" campaign.

---extended---
SiteX needed a web presence that reinforced their campaign message: dress like you mean it. I designed the full site and co-programmed it with a teammate. The standout feature is an interactive background switcher — three animal print patterns (zebra, peacock, leopard) that the user can toggle on the fly. It's a small detail that turns the site into an extension of the campaign, not just a brochure next to it.
