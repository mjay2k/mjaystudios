---extended---
The client wanted to communicate the vast mixability of PAMA. I took a show-don't-tell approach — dozens of unique cocktails, each professionally mixed and shot, composed into a single mosaic art piece around the bottle. The full composition lives inside a branded frame built on PAMA's pomegranate motif. The concept scaled across seasons — Valentine's Day, Holiday — same framework, fresh executions. Ran nationally in print, outdoor, and digital. Award-winning campaign.
