## Tone & Voice Guidelines

Based on our collaborative writing session, Matthew's preferred copy style is:

1. **Confident and direct.** No corporate-speak, no marketing fluff. Say what happened.
2. **Let the work speak.** Don't over-explain what the images already show.
3. **Don't describe process minutiae.** Nobody cares about the Photoshop comps. Show the thinking, not the tools.
4. **Don't fabricate briefs.** Don't write "The brief:" with made-up client quotes. Keep it real — "The client wanted X."
5. **Short > long.** If it can be said in one sentence, don't use three.
6. **No fake titles or inflated roles.** Describe what you did, not a title you didn't have.
7. **Results matter.** "Currently on shelves nationwide" beats "delivered a comprehensive packaging solution."
